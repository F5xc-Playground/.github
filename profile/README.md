# f5xc-playground

A place for F5ers to build stuff on top of Distributed Cloud — the things you wish existed, the ideas you hacked on over a long weekend, the tools that make your day a little less annoying.

Nothing here is official product. Everything here is someone solving their own problem.

### What belongs here

- Tools and integrations that make working with XC better
- Experiments and proofs of concept
- Things that started as "I wonder if I could..."

### What doesn't

- Anything with customer data or internal secrets (obviously)
- Repos that should live in an official F5 org

Want to move a project here or start something new? Reach out to the org admins.
